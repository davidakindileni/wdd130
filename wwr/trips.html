Scenic Float Trip

Designed for beginners, families, and those seeking a relaxing experience. The river section is calm with minimal rapids (Class I–II), allowing guests to enjoy wildlife, landscapes, and gentle paddling.

Half-Day Adventure

Short but exciting trips that pack in some moderate rapids (Class II–III). Great for travelers with limited time but who want a taste of adrenaline.

Full-Day Whitewater

Covers a longer stretch of river with multiple rapid sections, often ranging from Class III–IV. Includes breaks for lunch onshore. Suitable for adventurous beginners and intermediate paddlers.
