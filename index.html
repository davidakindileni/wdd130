Ile-Ife

Ile-Ife, in Osun State, Nigeria, is the legendary birthplace of the Yoruba civilization and a sacred city of immense historical significance.

Renowned for its ancient bronze and terracotta sculptures, it is home to the Ooni dynasty, the Oranmiyan Staff, and sacred groves central to Yoruba spirituality and tradition.
